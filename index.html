ABOUT US

AFS Agency was created four years ago by Mark Sadofsky. Mark has been in the lighting industry since 1978. After a short absence, he decided to return to his passion. Realizing there was a gap in the industry for representation of lighting lines for retail, electrical wholesalers as well as designers and architects, AFS Agency was launched.

In the fall of 2014, Mark partnered with Liz Moir to service both architects and designers with their comprehensive lighting lines. Liz has been in the lighting industry for 25 years and has certification with the American Lighting Association.

Projects include White Point Beach, Victory Arms, Onyx, Flip Burger as well as multiple apartments and multi use buildings.
